What is LuneOS?

luneOS is an Open-Source Continuation of webOS when Open webOS came out, the devs added Qt Support New webview technologies and new Service Calls, The Development started back in 2013, 10 years ago from now, the first release was called Affogato, the design is the same as used in the original webOS, luneOS brought some new system apps that werent on webOS and even a new Preware Revision was born, its called Preware 2 but this time its written in Enyo 2 which is the superior version of Enyo 1, it adds new features and design changes, the official latest release was called Eggnog Latte, it was released back in 2019 but no official release were made now, but a Discord User Named herrie.1982 still makes new luneOS Revisions, he migrated the base OS Kernel from open webOS to webOS OSE adding even more features and Android Apps Support using Waydroid based on Android 11, and luneOS is minimal-based on Android 9 now
